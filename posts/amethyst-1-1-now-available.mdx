---
title: Amethyst 1.1 now available
summary: Amethyst 1.1 is here with Amethyst Plus, custom reader views, widgets, writer following, Apple Watch support, and much more.
date: 2026-07-09 10:00:00 -0400
---

Amethyst 1.1 adds a plethora of features to truly enrich the experience and make the app feel much more complete. Here's what's new:

- Amethyst Plus now live! For unlimited push notifications, the ability to follow writers, and customization - you can subscribe to or purchase Amethyst Plus.
- New custom reader view on macOS and visionOS, no more direct web views! iOS and iPadOS still use the native Safari reader mode.
- Two new toggles for the reader: one to enable/disable reader views based on the source, and another to bypass opening in app entirely, in case you prefer your browser.
- Widgets, for both home screen and lock screen! You can set whatever feed you'd like to follow - whether that be a folder, a specific publication, all of your favorites, or a specific writer.
- On that note, you can also now follow writers! If you want to follow one author you really like from a publication - and only keep up with their work, now you can! You can see writers on individual feeds, and there's also a combined following feed for all of your followed writers.
- Apple Watch support! This comes with a complication that shows a headline from your specified source, similar to the iPhone lock screen widget. When you open an article from a complication (or a notification), it'll pass it to your iPhone with handoff so you can read it from there. You can customize the source in the iPhone app.
- Read individual feeds on iPhone: This feature was available on platforms with sidebar, but wasn't possible on iPhone until now. Now, read with more granularity.
- Notification improvements: more reliable, less prone to bugs that cause notification floods, image previews, syncing improvements, and the ability to mark feeds as time sensitive. As part of these improvements, favorites now have notifications enabled by default, though you can still disable notifications for a favorite in settings.
- Article images! Feeds now show thumbnail previews, and notifications do as well.
- Share sheet integration: You can now add a source to your feed or article to your bookmarks, straight from Safari.
- Early localization: English AU/CA/GB, Portuguese, Italian, French, Spanish, and German.
- Smaller improvements: added tips for better UI navigation, improved visionOS UI layout, ability to set theme colors, ability to delete articles from app, and scrolling improvements.

Thanks for using Amethyst!
