Introducing Amethyst: The feature-packed, easy to use RSS reader that I've always wanted!

Whether you're a news junkie or just a casual, I'm hoping Amethyst serves to be a fantastic tool for organizing your news consumption.

Add whatever feeds you'd like, organize them into groups, enable push notifications for the sources you'd like to hear from - and you're off to the races!

In the early days, Amethyst will be available completely for free - including push notifications - while I refine the experience and flesh things out. All feedback is welcome at my email contact@michaelb.nyc. A subscription for push notifications will likely be added in a later update depending on costs.

[Download Amethyst on the App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/amethyst-rss-reader/id6758023629) for iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Vision Pro. Supports iOS 18 and later - and it's free!
